SwipeHire is an AI-powered job and internship matchmaking platform that revolutionizes hiring through a swipe-based interface. The system leverages machine learning to analyze resumes, job descriptions, and engagement patterns, providing highly personalized recommendations for recruits and recruiters.

## Key Features

- **Bidirectional Swiping** – Mutual interest-based matching between candidates and recruiters.
- **AI-Powered Resume Analysis** – Uses **BERT-based NLP models** to extract key skills, experiences, and preferences.
- **Engagement-Based Ranking** – ML models analyze swipe patterns and interaction time to refine suggestions.
- **Collaborative Filtering** – AI-driven recommendations powered by **Neural Collaborative Filtering (NCF)** to suggest the best job or candidate match.
- **Automated Notifications** – Keeps users informed about new matches and application updates.

## System Architecture

The platform integrates a **Next.js** frontend, a **Supabase** backend for authentication and storage, and **machine learning models deployed via an API** for matchmaking intelligence.

## Workflow

1. **User Sign Up/Login** – Candidates and recruiters create accounts and log in.
2. **Profile Setup** – Users update resumes, tags, and preferences.
3. **Swiping Mechanism** – AI models analyze patterns to refine recommendations.
4. **Matchmaking & Suggestions** – The system identifies the best candidates/jobs using **Collaborative Filtering and BERT embeddings**.
5. **Notifications & Updates** – Users receive real-time updates on matches and recruiter interactions.

## AI Models Used

- **BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers)** – Extracts meaningful insights from resumes and job descriptions.
- **Neural Collaborative Filtering (NCF)** – Enhances recommendations based on mutual engagement.
- **Deep Factorization Machines (DeepFM)** – Improves ranking for job listings and candidate profiles.

SwipeHire streamlines the job-hunting and hiring process, making it more engaging and efficient for both recruits and recruiters.
